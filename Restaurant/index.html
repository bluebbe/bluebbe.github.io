Restaurant Description

The Double R Diner (for Railroad Diner) is the local diner in the town of Twin Peaks, Washington. It is owned and operated by Norma Jennings. It is a town institution and oftentimes a hang-out, specializing in homemade pie and coffee. It is open all days of the week and holidays. It is also a food provider for the town Meals on Wheels program and is a sponsor of the Miss Twin Peaks Contest.
